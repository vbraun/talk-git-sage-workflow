  Consider the quadratic equation
  \begin{equation}
    a x^2 + b x + c
  \end{equation}
  Then 
  \begin{equation}
    \label{eq:quad}
    x_{1,2} = \frac{-b+-\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}
  \end{equation}
  are the two roots of the quadratic equation.
